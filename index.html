HOW IT WORKS

Data Extraction

This tracker processes your Endfield pull history locally, entirely within your own browser. To use it, you must upload your pull history formatted as a .json file using the orange '+' button at the top of the interface.

For security and privacy reasons, this site fundamentally rejects and refuses any direct token uploads or gacha URLs. You are solely responsible for extracting your own token and generating the required JSON file. How you choose to extract that data—whether through third-party scripts, packet sniffers, or manual extraction—is entirely up to you.

Once you have securely extracted and saved your pull data locally as a `.json`, simply load it into the tracker to visualize your history.
